About — why it's free

Quality, Data, and Process Improvement Training

Built by a quality and process improvement professional with experience in engineering, project management, data analysis, Lean Six Sigma, and operational improvement. UpSkill Sprint Consulting exists for people who want practical skills but don't have the time, money, or access to expensive training programs. Every lesson is designed to be simple, useful, and immediately applicable at work — and it will always be free.

Vision

To make practical data and process improvement skills accessible to every worker, aspiring analyst, and new manager — so anyone can solve workplace problems, improve performance, and advance their career without cost or credential barriers.

Mission

We teach factory-floor workers, aspiring analysts, and new managers practical data analysis, process improvement, and business problem-solving through free, plain-language lessons, real workplace examples, downloadable tools, and hands-on datasets they can use immediately on the job.
